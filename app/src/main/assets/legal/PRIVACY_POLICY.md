# Privacy Policy

HatchBase ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information**: Email address and display name provided during authentication.
- **Flock Data**: Information you input about your birds, flocks, and incubations.
- **Financial Data**: Optional cost and revenue entries stored locally and synced to Firestore.
- **Usage Data**: Crashlytics and Analytics data to improve app performance.

## 2. Data Storage
Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Auth and Firestore). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 3. Your Rights
You can export or delete your account and all associated data at any time via the User Profile settings.

## 4. Updates
This policy may be updated from time to time. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the latest version.
