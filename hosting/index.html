Explore the core tools inside HatchBase

These are the app areas poultry keepers use every day to manage birds, eggs, growth, breeding plans, and business performance in one connected system.

Flock

Organize adult birds by flock, keep breed and purpose details together, and maintain a cleaner record of the birds you are actively managing.

Incubation

Set up batches, follow milestones, and track progress from the first egg through lockdown and hatch day.

Nursery

Keep an eye on chicks after hatch, track early development, and manage the handoff from hatch results into growing birds.

Egg Production

Log daily egg counts, note cracked or reserved eggs, and understand how production is feeding incubation, sales, and flock performance.

Breeding

Compare pairings, follow breeding goals, and keep long-term improvement work visible instead of relying on scattered notes.

Finance

Review costs, revenue, and profitability so you can connect everyday flock decisions to the financial side of your operation.

From eggs to adult birds
