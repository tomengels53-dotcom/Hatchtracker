Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

HatchBase ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or contact customer support. This may include your email address and profile information. We also store data you generate within the app, such as egg incubation records, flock details, and breeding scenarios.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including syncing your data across devices, processing subscriptions, and providing customer support.

3. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. You can do this directly within the application settings using the "Delete Account" feature. This action is permanent and will remove all your records from our servers.

Need the exact deletion steps? Visit Account and Data Deletion.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hatchbase.io.
