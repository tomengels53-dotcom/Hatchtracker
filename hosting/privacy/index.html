Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

HatchBase ("we", "our", or "us") explains here how we collect, use, and share information when you use the HatchBase Android app and the public HatchBase website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and profile data: Authentication details such as your email address, display name, authentication provider identifiers, preferred language, country and currency settings, and legal-consent version records.
  • App data you create: Records you enter for flocks, birds, incubations, egg production, finance, breeding plans, devices, maintenance logs, and related operational data.
  • Support data: Support tickets, ticket messages, internal triage results, device details, app version, diagnostics, and other information you send when requesting help.
  • Notification data: Firebase Cloud Messaging token data, app version, and token activity timestamps used to deliver push notifications.
  • Subscription and billing data: Subscription status, product identifiers, expiry dates, and related purchase-verification data used to manage paid access. Public Android app subscriptions are currently processed through Google Play.
  • Telemetry and reliability data: Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and app telemetry metadata such as app version, build environment, network state, device class, Android version, and session identifiers.
  • Advertising data: Free-tier users may receive Google AdMob ads, including rewarded ads that can temporarily suppress ads for a period stored locally on device.

2. How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • authenticate users and maintain accounts;
  • sync app data across devices;
  • deliver reminders, push notifications, and support workflows;
  • verify subscriptions and enforce feature access;
  • diagnose crashes, performance issues, and support requests;
  • improve the app and protect the service from abuse.

3. Storage and Service Providers

We use Google Firebase services, including Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging, Analytics, and Crashlytics. We also use Google Play Billing for the public Android subscription flow and Google AdMob for advertising in eligible app experiences.

Support and operational alerts may create email-processing records used to route support notifications. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Website

The public HatchBase website is primarily a static marketing and legal-information site. It currently does not provide public user accounts, checkout, community posting, or marketplace activity. If you contact us through a mail link, your email provider and our support inbox will process that message.

5. Features Not Yet Public

Community and marketplace modules exist in development code but are not currently accessible to public users. They are not part of the current public HatchBase service. If those features are launched publicly, we will update this policy before or at launch.

6. Your Choices and Deletion

You can request account deletion from the app. Deleting your HatchBase account does not cancel an active Google Play subscription; subscriptions must be managed separately in Google Play.

If you participated in pre-release or internal testing of unreleased modules, contact support for review of any test data that may require manual cleanup.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy or deletion requests can be sent to support@hatchbase.io.
